Crystal Collector!

Directions

-You will be given a random # at the start of the game.

-There are 4 crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specifc amount of points to your total score.

-You win the game by matching your total score to random #, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random #.

-The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

-Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
